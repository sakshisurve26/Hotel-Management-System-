# Hotel-Management-System-

The **Hotel Management System** is a web-based application developed to automate and streamline the day-to-day operations of a hotel. Managing hotel activities manually often leads to delays, data inconsistencies, and human errors. To overcome these challenges, this system provides a centralized platform where hotel staff can efficiently manage room bookings, customer records, billing processes, staff information, and hotel services. Developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the application offers an interactive and user-friendly interface that simplifies hotel administration and improves overall operational efficiency.

The system is divided into several modules, each responsible for a specific hotel function. The dashboard provides an overview of hotel performance and room occupancy, while the room management module allows staff to add, update, and monitor room availability. The booking management module enables reservation creation, automatic calculation of stay duration, and tracking of booking status throughout the guest's stay. These modules work together to ensure smooth coordination between different hotel operations and provide real-time updates whenever changes occur.

**Major Modules Included:**

* Dashboard Management
* Room Management
* Booking Management
* Check-In and Check-Out Management
* Billing and Invoice Generation
* Customer Management
* Staff Management
* Service Management (Laundry, Spa, Room Service)

One of the key features of the system is its ability to automate hotel workflows and maintain accurate records. Whenever a room is booked, checked in, or checked out, the room status is updated automatically. The billing module calculates room charges, additional service costs, and taxes to generate accurate invoices. Customer information and booking histories are stored systematically, making data retrieval simple and efficient. The modular architecture also makes the application organized, easy to maintain, and scalable for future enhancements.

The project was successfully implemented and tested, producing reliable results across all modules. The system provides real-time room availability updates, accurate billing calculations, efficient customer and staff management, and a responsive user interface. The use of JavaScript objects as an in-memory database effectively demonstrates data management concepts without requiring a dedicated backend. Although the current version does not support permanent data storage or online deployment, it successfully showcases the core functionalities of a real-world hotel management solution.

**Key Outcomes of the Project:**

* Improved operational efficiency
* Reduced manual effort and human errors
* Real-time room availability tracking
* Accurate billing and invoice generation
* Better management of customer and staff records
* Strong foundation for future industry-level development

In conclusion, the Hotel Management System demonstrates how modern web technologies can be used to automate and optimize hotel operations. The project highlights important concepts of web development, data management, and system design while providing a practical solution for hotel administration. Future enhancements such as database integration, online booking facilities, payment gateway support, cloud deployment, and AI-based recommendations can further transform it into a complete commercial hotel management platform
